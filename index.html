About ExoLiX

ExoLiX is an AI-powered platform designed for the 2025 NASA Space Apps Challenge. Our mission is to democratize exoplanet research by providing an accessible interface for training and deploying machine learning models on NASA's open-source datasets.

Built with TensorFlow.js, our system runs entirely in your browser, making it fast, private, and accessible to researchers and enthusiasts alike. Whether you're a seasoned astronomer or just curious about exoplanets, ExoLiX provides the tools you need to explore and analyze data from NASA's Kepler, K2, and TESS missions.

Data Sources

Our platform utilizes the NASA Exoplanet Archive's cumulative dataset, containing detailed observations of Kepler Objects of Interest including orbital periods, transit durations, planetary radii, stellar characteristics, and disposition classifications.
